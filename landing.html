Our story.

I'm Sydney. I've been to 27 countries, and have barely made a dent in my bucket list. For years I've struggled with an overwhelming travel wishlist and no real system to manage it. Planning a trip means hours of searching, saving, and starting over. Because I change my mind constantly, my itineraries become a mess of outdated plans, wrong dates, and suggestions that no longer make sense.

I'm building Vaia because I needed her to exist. And I think you do too.
